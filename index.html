About Our Shop

They say one picture is worth a thousand words. With our trendiest t-shirt, no need for a single word. Each time you step into these streets, the world listens. From Bergville to Ballito, we know our tribe, we know our stripes, that’s TSHIRT CULCHA. This is a concepts-centric brand and an accessory merchant creating trendy everyday t-shirts and marketing complementary accessories and lifestyle products.

To keep it moving, we have collaborated with cool kids around your block who keeps our staff and can swiftly make it available to you. Before you realize it, there could be Pop Up stores at the town near you. We’re open to branching to other parts of the country and making the brand available beyond our home soil, KwaZulu Natal.

Place an order now and let one of our merchants get in touch with you. We know you can’t wait to have your shirt, we get out of the way to ensure your order reaches you in 4 to 7 working days, once your order has been finalized. But if you’re much! Much! nearer, we’ll catch a taxi and drop your order to you, in 72 hours at worst. 

OUR ORDERING PROCESS

We pride ourselves with a fast and effective product acquisition process on all our merchandise. Here are the affected steps below.

1

Choose your items

Once you’ve picked your items and sent and enquiry to our office, we’ll reach via Email/WhatsApp to confirm your order to ensure everything is actually in order.

2

PAYMENT

You’ll receive a payment link/account details with which you will use to make your payment and send your proof of payment to money@tshirtculcha.co.za or via WhatsApp at 067 317 3929
3

DELIVERY

Once payment has been received, you will receive an SMS confirming the shipment of your order, pick up spot and estimated arrival date. 

Done! Thereafter,
